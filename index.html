How we work

Three steps.
No black boxes.

The same order, every time — because skipping a step is usually where automation projects go wrong.

STEP 10¹

Identify

We map how your business actually runs today and mark exactly which processes are worth automating first.

STEP 10²

Automate

We build the automation and walk you through exactly how it works — nothing you can't open up and understand.

STEP 10³

Improve

We stay on after launch with an open feedback loop, tuning the system as your business changes shape.

There are businesses, and there are the processes that run them. We're the partners who automate the parts that are already defined — so you're free to spend your time on the parts that aren't.

logbase10 exists on the idea that automation should feel like multiplication, not disruption. A logarithm turns a huge, unwieldy number into something you can actually work with — that's what we try to do with the busywork inside a growing business.

We identify the processes worth automating, build the automation, and give you a complete, plain-language understanding of how it works. Then we stick around: an open feedback loop means the system keeps getting sharper as your business does.

We also build and customise the AI itself — assistants trained on your data, models tuned to your requirements, support that's there whenever you need it, not just at kickoff.
